I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in the Decision, Risk, and Operations (DRO) division at Columbia Business School, where I am fortunate to be advised by [Hongyao Ma](https://www.hongyaoma.com/) and [Will Ma](https://www.columbia.edu/~wm2428/).

My research interests center on algorithmic decision-making under uncertainty, combining domain-specific design with theoretical performance guarantees to improve the operations of digital marketplaces such as on-demand delivery platforms.

Previously, I graduated as a mathematical engineer and an MS in Operations Management at Universidad de Chile, being advised by [José Correa](https://www.dii.uchile.cl/~jcorrea/) for my thesis.
